# Contributing

Contributions are welcome if they improve safe, reusable legal workflow design.

## Good Contributions

- sanitized examples
- better workflow scoring criteria
- safer asset creation rules
- new templates or checklists
- clearer confidentiality guidance
- translation improvements

## Do Not Contribute

- real client names
- live matter details
- contract text under confidentiality obligations
- privileged legal advice
- personal information
- API keys or secrets
- proprietary playbooks unless authorized

## Contribution Checklist

- [ ] The contribution is sanitized.
- [ ] No privileged or confidential facts are included.
- [ ] Examples use neutral placeholders.
- [ ] The legal boundary is clear.
- [ ] Human review points are included where appropriate.

## Review Standard

Maintainers should reject contributions that turn the project into a legal advice generator or embed matter-specific legal conclusions.
